TeA CuP

Rwandan tea growing regions are endowed with ideal climate; tropical, volcanic soils and well distributed rainfall ranging between 1200 mm to 1400mm per annum. There are also sunny days which are some of the climatic features of the Tea growing regions. Tea production labor is manual and much of the tea grown in Rwanda is processed using the Crush, Tear, Curl (CTC) method, making it suitable for use in blends popular in most black-tea markets.
